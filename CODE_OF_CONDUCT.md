# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
The summary below is informational; the linked text is authoritative.

## Our pledge / 私たちの誓い

We pledge to make participation in the AutonoMath project a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste,
colour, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

AutonoMath コミュニティに貢献する人すべてを尊重し、ハラスメントのない参加環境を維持します。
年齢・性別・宗教・国籍・性的指向・障害の有無を問わず、誰もが安心して
issue / PR / discussion に参加できることを目指します。

## Expected behaviour / 期待される行動

- Demonstrate empathy and kindness toward other people.
- Be respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Accept responsibility, apologise to those affected by mistakes, and
  learn from the experience.
- Focus on what is best for the overall community.

- 他者に対する敬意と配慮
- 異なる視点・経験への寛容
- 建設的なフィードバックの授受
- 自らの誤りを認め、影響を受けた人に謝罪し、経験から学ぶ
- コミュニティ全体の利益を優先する

## Unacceptable behaviour / 期待されない行動

- The use of sexualised language or imagery, and sexual attention or
  advances of any kind.
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or
  political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information (such as a physical or email
  address) without their explicit permission.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in
  a professional setting.

- 性的・暴力的な言動
- トローリング、侮辱、人格攻撃
- 公開・私的を問わない嫌がらせ
- 同意なしに他者の個人情報を公開すること
- 業務上不適切と一般的に判断される行為

## Scope / 適用範囲

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (GitHub repo:
issues, PRs, discussions, commit messages) and also applies when an
individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

GitHub repo (issue / PR / discussion / commit message)、AutonoMath の公式チャネル、
関連イベントすべてに適用されます。

## Enforcement / 報告と対応

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour
may be reported privately to **info@bookyou.net**. All complaints will
be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

問題行動を目撃または受けた場合、`info@bookyou.net` に直接ご連絡ください。
Bookyou 株式会社 (T8010001213708) 代表 (梅田茂利) が一次対応します。
報告内容は秘匿し、報告者のプライバシーを保護します。

### Enforcement guidelines

Following the Contributor Covenant 2.1 enforcement ladder:

1. **Correction** — A private, written warning, providing clarity
   around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the
   behaviour was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.
2. **Warning** — A warning with consequences for continued behaviour.
   No interaction with the people involved for a specified period.
3. **Temporary ban** — A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or
   public communication with the community for a specified period.
4. **Permanent ban** — A permanent ban from any sort of public
   interaction within the community.
