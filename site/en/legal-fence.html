Legal fence

jpcite returns public-source Evidence Packets and output artifacts; it does not provide tax, legal, audit, filing, registration, labor, intellectual-property, or labor-standards advice.

Boundary summary

  • API responses stay inside evidence/output retrieval and include disclaimers for sensitive professional-law areas.
  • The fence covers tax accountant, attorney, CPA, administrative scrivener / gyoseishoshi domains (Administrative Scriveners Act Articles 1-2 and 19), judicial scrivener, labor/social-security attorney, patent-attorney, and labor-standards domains.
  • For the live Japanese legal-fence page, see the Japanese page. For English FDI context, see jpcite for foreign-invested entities.
