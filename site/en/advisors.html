Evidence-to-Expert Handoff

For licensed Japanese professionals — 認定経営革新等支援機関 (certified SME management innovation support organizations), tax accountants (税理士), labor & social-security attorneys (社労士), and SME management consultants (中小企業診断士). jpcite prepares a source-linked evidence brief before professional review when SMEs need help with subsidy or financing applications.

Professional registration

To join the handoff cohort, email info@bookyou.net with subject "jpcite handoff professional application" and attach your registration certificate (登録通知 or 登録証). Replies are sent in Japanese; the underlying data and matching engine are language-neutral.

How the handoff works

  • End-users (SMEs / sole proprietors) ask a subsidy or financing question through public web/API evaluation or Playground.
  • jpcite prepares an evidence brief with source URLs, fetched_at timestamps, known gaps, and next checks before professional review.
  • When the issue needs licensed review, jpcite matches by region, industry, and engagement size, then surfaces eligible professionals.
  • If the user retains you, they contract directly with you at your published rate. jpcite does not take a margin on your professional fee.
  • Customer rating disclosure is required after each engagement (景表法 compliant).

Cost

  • End-user (the SME): ¥0 to ask for an evidence brief and professional-review handoff. They pay your professional fee directly if they retain you.
  • Professional listing: free to register, ¥0 monthly. Pay ¥3,000 tax exclusive (¥3,300 tax inclusive) only when a client actually retains you. No subscription, no minimum spend, no exclusivity.

Full pricing detail is on the Japanese handoff page.

Eligibility

To list, you must hold one of: 認定経営革新等支援機関 ID, 税理士登録番号, 社会保険労務士登録番号, or 中小企業診断士登録番号. Foreign-licensed equivalents are not eligible — this is a Japan-jurisdiction professional service.
