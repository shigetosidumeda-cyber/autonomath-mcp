Primary use cases

1. Restaurant owner: "What subsidies are available in Tokyo?"

Send prefecture and industry to LINE. You'll see Tokyo SME Promotion Public Corporation grants, the small-business sustainability subsidy, and IT-introduction subsidies (POS / order systems), each with the source URL and application deadline.

2. Manufacturer (~10 staff): "Subsidies for capital investment"

Top 5 includes monozukuri subsidy (SME Support Japan), business-restructuring subsidy (METI), and energy-saving subsidy (MOE). The exclusion-rule view tells you "this and that can't be combined".

3. Sole proprietor: "Special measures for tax filing"

Surfaces blue-form filing special deduction, the Small Enterprise Mutual Aid system, iDeCo, and similar measures. Pulls relevant rulesets from the 35 NTA-sourced tax_rulesets.

4. Retailer: "Japan Finance Corporation loan terms"

Out of 108 JFC products, surfaces loans that match your industry and size. Transparent across three axes: collateral / personal guarantor / third-party guarantor.

5. From application question to professional advisor

Send "I'd like help filing" in LINE and we return a list of advisors that match your region and industry. Compare profiles, specialties, and rate cards, then contact directly.

FAQ (SMB owners)

Do I need to sign up?

Just add the LINE bot as a friend. No app, no email registration. The first 10 questions per month don't even require a card.

What kinds of questions can I ask?

Natural-language phrases work: "subsidies for restaurants in Tokyo", "tax incentives for IT in manufacturing", "grants for retailers with 5 employees". Including prefecture and industry improves accuracy.

Am I billed from question 11?

Yes. Question 11 onward is ¥3 per question (¥3.30 tax inclusive). Pay each time with LINE Pay / Apple Pay / Google Pay, or register a card with Stripe for monthly settlement. Counter resets at JST first-of-month 00:00.

Are answers AI-generated?

AutonoMath returns matching programs from its database (13,578 programs) using rules — no creative writing, no embellishment. Source URLs are always attached.

Who do I talk to about actually filing?

Tap "I'd like to consult" in LINE and we send a list of certified support organizations, tax advisors, and administrative scriveners that match your region and industry. Direct contracting with the advisor (the advisor sets the price).

How do I cancel?

Send "cancel" in LINE, or remove the card from your Stripe portal. No termination fees, and you can resume anytime.

Does this work for sole proprietors?

Yes. Both incorporated SMBs and sole proprietors are supported. Filter target_types=sole_proprietor and you'll see income-tax filing programs and the small-business sustainability subsidy first.
