Primary use cases

1. Restaurant owner: "What subsidies are available in Tokyo?"

Enter prefecture and industry in the web prescreen. You'll see Tokyo SME Promotion Public Corporation grants, the small-business sustainability subsidy, and IT-introduction subsidies (POS / order systems), each with the source URL and application deadline.

2. Manufacturer (~10 staff): "Subsidies for capital investment"

Top 5 includes monozukuri subsidy (SME Support Japan), business-restructuring subsidy (METI), and energy-saving subsidy (MOE). The exclusion-rule view tells you "this and that can't be combined".

3. Sole proprietor: "Special measures for tax filing"

Surfaces blue-form filing special deduction, the Small Enterprise Mutual Aid system, iDeCo, and similar measures. Pulls relevant records from NTA-sourced tax-rule data.

4. Retailer: "Japan Finance Corporation loan terms"

Out of 108 JFC products, surfaces loans that match your industry and size. Transparent across three axes: collateral / personal guarantor / third-party guarantor.

5. From application question to professional advisor

Use the prescreen result as a consultation pack, then contact an advisor that matches your region and industry. Compare profiles, specialties, and rate cards before contracting directly.

FAQ (SMB owners)

Do I need to sign up?

Use the Playground / API evaluation path for searches. Saved-search and deadline reminders can be received by email.

What kinds of questions can I ask?

Natural-language phrases work: "subsidies for restaurants in Tokyo", "tax incentives for IT in manufacturing", "grants for retailers with 5 employees". Including prefecture and industry improves accuracy.

Can I pay for questions by email?

No. Email is a reminder channel. Searches and paid usage use the API / Playground path listed on Pricing.

Are answers AI-generated?

jpcite returns matching programs from its database (14,472 programs) using rules — no creative writing, no embellishment. Source URLs are always attached.

Who do I talk to about actually filing?

Use the prescreen result and handoff notes to contact certified support organizations, tax advisors, and administrative scriveners that match your region and industry. Direct contracting with the advisor (the advisor sets the price).

How do I stop email notices?

Email reminders include an unsubscribe path, and notification settings can be managed from the related account flow.

Does this work for sole proprietors?

Yes. Both incorporated SMBs and sole proprietors are supported. Filter target_type=sole_proprietor and you'll see income-tax filing programs and the small-business sustainability subsidy first.

Related audiences?

Find a professional to file: tax advisors, administrative scriveners. Tech side: AI agent developers (embed in your bot), VC / M&A advisors (target screening). Compare all 5 audiences.
