Cross-references

Cases that move between the two through client referrals. Tax advisors specialize in special measures and filings; administrative scriveners handle permits and subsidy paperwork. Both tax advisor and administrative scrivener pages map the overlap.

SMB → professional advisor

SMB owners discover programs via public web/API evaluation, then consent to connect with a matched professional when needed. Free for the user; the ¥3,300 is paid by the professional per closed introduction. SMB → home.

VC ↔ AI developer

DD pipelines need compact source-linked evidence before report drafting. The VC defines requirements; the developer wires up MCP / REST. Combine the VC and developer pages.

Tax advisor → AI developer

Firms building internal AI review workflows plug the MCP server straight into Claude Desktop or Cursor. Tax advisor → developer.
