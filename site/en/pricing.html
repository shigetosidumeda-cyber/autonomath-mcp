When NOT to use us

jpcite is an information-only service. If any of the following apply, please use a different option.

  • You need tax representation, tax advice, or filing on your behalf — prohibited under 税理士法 §52. Please consult a registered tax accountant or 社労士.
  • You need real-time statute updates — source updates are scheduled and can lag by up to 24 hours. For instant amendment reflection, refer to e-Gov or 官報 directly.
  • You want adoption-rate guarantees — we display the rates that public agencies publish; we do not predict or guarantee adoption. The application decision rests with the applicant.
  • You require custom contracts or quotes — fully self-serve only. The published price applies to every customer equally.

FAQ

Are there tiers?

No. ¥3 per billable unit only — no Free / Starter / Pro / Enterprise. Every customer pays the same unit rate.

Volume discount for high usage?

No. 100 units or 1,000,000 units per month — every customer pays ¥3 per billable unit (¥3.30 tax inclusive). No sales negotiation, no custom quotes.

Is signup required?

No. The first 3 req/day per IP are free without signup (resets at JST next-day 00:00). After the anonymous allowance, issue an API key + register a card in Stripe.

Cancellation procedure?

Just delete the API key in the dashboard, or remove the card in Stripe Customer Portal. No cancellation fee, no notice period, no retention call. Reverts to anonymous 3 req/day immediately.

Stripe auto-billing? Qualified Japanese invoice?

Yes. End-of-month metered usage is auto-charged on day 1 of the following month. Stripe Tax issues a qualified Japanese invoice with the required issuer details.

Corporations and sole proprietors?

Both. Same ¥3/billable unit for taxable / tax-exempt entities, corporations, and sole proprietors. Every invoice carries the required qualified-invoice details for input-tax-credit handling.

Can I set a monthly spend cap?

Yes. Set monthly_cap_yen from the dashboard (or POST /v1/me/cap). Once hit, the API returns 503 with cap_reached until the next JST monthly billing cycle. For broad batch or fanout workflows, also use X-Cost-Cap-JPY, Idempotency-Key, and X-Client-Tag to control request budget, retries, and customer/project attribution.

How is a billable unit defined?

Normal HTTP / MCP search and detail calls are 1 billable unit. Batch, export, and bulk-evaluation endpoints may bill multiple units based on deduped IDs or bundle size; those formulas are shown before use. /v1/healthz and /v1/me/* meta endpoints are uncounted.
