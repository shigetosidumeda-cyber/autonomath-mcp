Notifications

Current notifications start with email. jpcite search and evidence generation live in REST/MCP, Playground, dashboard workflows, and the embeddable widget; notifications are reminders and update channels, not a paid questioning surface.

Status: email notification requests are open

For live search, use the REST/MCP API, Playground, or the embeddable widget. To receive saved-search and deadline notifications, email info@bookyou.net.

What the channel is for

  • Saved-search reminders when a relevant public-program window changes.
  • Advisory-pack updates when new evidence, known gaps, or questions are added.
  • Deadline reminders that route the user back to source-backed web or API results.
  • Human review handoff when a result needs a licensed professional or advisor workflow.

Pricing

  • REST/MCP and normal search pricing remains on the Pricing page.
  • Current API, Playground, and widget pricing is listed on the Pricing page; paid notification terms are shown before use.
  • No visitor-facing payment is required just to view public search results or source links.

Why this exists

Many users do the real work in a client file, dashboard, or website widget, but need a short reminder when a deadline or evidence update matters. This page exists to explain that notification layer clearly without implying that a public LINE search bot is already the main product.
