Notifications

Current notifications start with email. They are reminder and update channels for saved searches, deadlines, and consultation packs, not a paid questioning surface.

Status: email notification requests are open

  • Notification terms are shown before any paid notification flow.

Pricing

Current API, Playground, and widget pricing is listed on the Pricing page; paid notification terms are shown before use.

Why notifications

SME owners and licensed advisors may not want to revisit a dashboard. Notifications are useful for reminders and handoff updates after a source-linked prescreen has been created elsewhere.
