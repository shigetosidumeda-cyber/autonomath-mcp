LINE notifications

LINE is being prepared as a notification and reminder channel for saved searches, deadline reminders, and consultation-pack updates. It is not a live paid questioning surface.

Status: preparing for public availability

Please use the REST/MCP API or the embeddable widget while LINE availability is being announced. To receive a notification, email info@bookyou.net with subject "jpcite LINE notification".

Planned workflow

  • Run searches through the REST/MCP API, Playground, or the embeddable widget.
  • Save the programs, source URLs, open questions, and consultation notes you want to track.
  • When LINE becomes available, receive reminders for saved-search changes, deadlines, and advisor handoff updates.
  • Future LINE usage terms will be shown before launch.

Pricing

LINE paid usage is not live. Current API, Playground, and widget pricing is listed on the Pricing page; any LINE terms will be shown before public launch.

Why LINE

In Japan, LINE has roughly 95M monthly active users, including SME owners and licensed advisors who may not want to revisit a dashboard. That makes it useful for reminders and handoff updates after a source-linked prescreen has been created elsewhere.
