How revenue share works

A single mechanism applies to every partner. Full spec: partner_referral_mechanism.md.

Referral mechanism summary
Item Detail
Per-user price ¥3 / req (excl. tax; ¥3.30 incl. tax). Referral channels pay the same price — discounts are never granted.
Referral share 10% of metered revenue (Anthropic Directory is the only exception at 0%).
Payout method Stripe Connect Transfer or domestic bank transfer. No minimum payout.
Cycle Monthly close at 23:59 JST on the last day of the month; payout 17:00 JST on the last day of the following month.
Request count Only billable, successful requests made through paid API keys. The anonymous 50 req/month free quota and 4xx responses are excluded.
Qualified invoice 税務会計AI (Bookyou Inc., T8010001213708) issues a payment notice that doubles as a 適格請求書 (qualified invoice).
Penalties / minimum term None. A month with 0 req simply skips the transfer (¥0 payout).

Worked example

A user acquired through the freee Marketplace runs 20,000 requests in one month:

request_count   = 20,000
charged_jpy     = 20,000 × ¥3 = ¥60,000  (excl. tax)
share_percent   = 10
share_jpy       = floor(¥60,000 × 0.10) = ¥6,000  ← paid to freee
税務会計AI net  = ¥54,000  (excl. tax)
