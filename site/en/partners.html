How revenue share works

A single mechanism applies to every partner. Pricing source of truth: Pricing.

Referral mechanism summary
Item Detail
Per-user price ¥3 / unit (excl. tax; ¥3.30 incl. tax). Referral channels pay the same price — discounts are never granted.
Referral share 10% of metered revenue (Anthropic Directory is the only exception at 0%).
Payout method Stripe Connect Transfer or domestic bank transfer. No minimum payout.
Cycle Monthly close at 23:59 JST on the last day of the month; payout 17:00 JST on the last day of the following month.
Request count Only billable, successful requests made through paid API keys. The anonymous 3 req/day free quota and 4xx responses are excluded.
Qualified invoice jpcite (Bookyou K.K. / Bookyou株式会社) issues a payment notice that includes qualified-invoice information where applicable.
Penalties / minimum term None. A month with 0 req simply skips the transfer (¥0 payout).

Worked example

A user acquired through the freee Marketplace runs 20,000 requests in one month:

request_count = 20,000
charged_jpy = 20,000 × ¥3 = ¥60,000 (excl. tax)
share_percent = 10
share_jpy = floor(¥60,000 × 0.10) = ¥6,000 ← paid to freee
jpcite net = ¥54,000 (excl. tax)

Partnership principles

  • Self-serve only. Partnerships are handled through self-service docs and email; separate enterprise contracts are not offered at this time.
  • ¥3/billable unit, fully uniform. Partner channels do not unlock discounts — the referral fee is absorbed by jpcite as a cost.
  • Brand names are listed as potential integration targets. No partnership or endorsement is implied unless explicitly announced.
  • Pass-through data. Accounting, HR, or record data flowing in via partner channels is never persisted on jpcite servers — it is consumed as a query parameter and discarded.
  • Support. Support is handled through documentation and email. Standard terms apply unless otherwise published.
  • Organic only. No advertising, no sales activity. Partnerships are inbound self-serve. The "Featured Partner" label is only used after explicit consent (景表法 compliance).
