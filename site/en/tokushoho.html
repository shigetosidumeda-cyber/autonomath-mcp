Specified Commercial Transactions Act disclosure

Last updated: 2026-05-14

The Specified Commercial Transactions Act (特定商取引法) disclosure is available in Japanese at /tokushoho.html. The statute governs the Japanese-language version, so jpcite does not publish an English translation. For a plain-English summary for due diligence, email info@bookyou.net.

Plain-English summary for due diligence: paid API/MCP use is metered at JPY 3 per billable unit tax exclusive (JPY 3.30 tax inclusive); normal search/detail calls are one billable unit, while batch/export/fanout endpoints may consume multiple units according to formulas shown before use. Billing is monthly through Stripe. Cancellation is available from the Stripe Customer Portal; current-month usage remains billable, and API key deletion or rotation is not billing cancellation. Digital-service charges are not refundable after service begins except for confirmed billing errors or confirmed material defects. Statutory cooling-off does not apply to this online service.
